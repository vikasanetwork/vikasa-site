About VIKASA

VIKASA NETWORK (VIK) is a community-driven rewards token built for transparent utility, staking, and governance. Holders earn benefits through decentralized participation and the upcoming Ecosystem App.

Ecosystem Development

  • 2025: Token verification and official logo update on PolygonScan.
  • 2025: Launch of the Vikasa Ecosystem App for users — staking, tracking, and rewards dashboard.
  • 2026: Liquidity addition and 2-year lock for stability and transparency.
  • 2026: Integration and listing on CoinGecko and CoinMarketCap.
  • 2026: Initial listings on selected small exchanges to expand accessibility.
