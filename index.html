About VIKASA

         

VIKASA NETWORK (VIK) is a community-driven rewards token designed for transparent utility, staking, and governance. Holders earn benefits through decentralized participation and real-world integrations within the Vikasa ecosystem. Our focus is on building a robust, secure foundation on **Polygon's Proof-of-Stake** network, ensuring fast and low-cost transactions for all users.
