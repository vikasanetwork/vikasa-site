Advertising — Google AdMob

VIKASA Network uses Google AdMob to show rewarded video ads. Watching ads is entirely optional and rewards you with bonus VIK tokens.

What AdMob Collects

  • Advertising ID (GAID on Android)
  • IP address and approximate location (country level)
  • App usage and ad interaction data
  • Device make, model, and OS version

How to Opt Out of Personalised Ads

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID
  • Or enable "Opt out of Ads Personalisation" in your device Google settings
📱 Even if you opt out of personalised ads, you will still see ads in VIKASA Network — they will just be non-personalised. Ads are clearly labelled before they play.

User profiles, balances, historySupabase (PostgreSQL)AWS ap-south-1 (Mumbai, India)

Security Measures

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS / TLS 1.2+ encryption
  • Passwords are never stored — Firebase handles authentication only
  • Database access requires authenticated JWT tokens — no public access
  • Firebase Security Rules enforce per-user data isolation
  • No sensitive data stored on device (uses secure token storage)
⚠️ While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

Children's Privacy

VIKASA Network is intended for users aged 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 years of age.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at the email address below. We will delete such information within 7 days of confirmation.
