KYC Verification

VIKASA Network includes a KYC (Know Your Customer) verification process. When you submit KYC, you provide:

  • A photo of a government-issued identity document (e.g., Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Driving Licence)
  • A selfie or face photograph for liveness verification

How KYC Data Is Handled

  • KYC documents are submitted voluntarily and used solely for identity verification
  • Documents are stored securely and accessible only to authorised personnel
  • KYC status (pending / approved / rejected) is stored in your account
  • We do not share your identity documents with any third party for commercial purposes

Why KYC Is Required

KYC verification is required to unlock advanced features on the platform, including (in future phases) withdrawals and higher reward tiers. This is in line with applicable Indian regulations on identity verification for digital reward and financial services platforms.

🔒
Your identity documents are sensitive. We take their security seriously. They are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections and stored with restricted access controls. You may request deletion of your KYC documents by contacting us.

Advertising — Google AdMob

VIKASA Network uses Google AdMob to display rewarded video ads in the Earn section. Watching ads is entirely voluntary — it rewards you with additional VIK tokens.

What AdMob Collects

  • Android Advertising ID (GAID)
  • IP address and approximate location (country level only)
  • App usage and ad interaction data
  • Device make, model, and OS version

Opt Out of Personalised Ads

  • Go to: Android Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID
  • Or enable: "Opt out of Ads Personalisation"
📱
If you opt out of personalised ads, you will still see ads in VIKASA Network — they will be non-personalised. All ads are clearly labelled before they begin playing.

Security Measures

  • KYC documents stored with restricted bucket-level access policies
  • No sensitive data cached unencrypted on the device
  • Secure token storage used for session management on device

While we implement industry-standard security, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but we take every reasonable precaution to protect your data.
